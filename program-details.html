DreamTech Startup Fund Program

DreamTech proudly supports graduates by providing immediate access to startup capital, mentorship, and job placement opportunities in the tech industry.

Students who rank in the top 5 of their cohort receive an additional $1,000 performance bonus, while students ranking 6th to 10th receive a $500 bonus. For all other graduates, a base startup grant of $1,400 is awarded.

All DreamTech graduates are automatically introduced into the digital workforce and paired with industry mentors who guide them into professional roles within software, cybersecurity, UI/UX, AI operations, and other high-demand fields.

This program aims to empower students with tools, funding, and connections needed to thrive as tech professionals or founders.
